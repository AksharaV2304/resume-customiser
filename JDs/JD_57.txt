 Job Description: Retail CCAR Model Auditor

Position Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Retail CCAR Model Auditor to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in risk management, financial modeling, and data analysis with proficiency in R programming language.

Responsibilities:
1. Perform comprehensive audits of retail Credit Concentration Analysis and Reporting (CCAR) models to ensure accuracy, compliance, and effectiveness.
2. Collaborate with model developers, risk management teams, and senior management to understand the underlying assumptions, methodologies, and data sources used in CCAR models.
3. Validate the robustness, reliability, and consistency of CCAR models across various scenarios and stress tests.
4. Utilize R programming language for data analysis, modeling, and automation tasks related to CCAR audits.
5. Prepare detailed reports summarizing findings, recommendations, and any identified issues or concerns with the CCAR models.
6. Work closely with model developers to implement changes and improvements based on audit findings.
7. Stay current with industry trends, regulations, and best practices in risk management and financial modeling.
8. Participate in ad-hoc projects and initiatives as needed.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, or a related field.
2. Proven experience in CCAR model auditing or risk management within the financial services industry.
3. Strong proficiency in R programming language and experience using R for data analysis and modeling tasks.
4. Excellent analytical skills, problem-solving abilities, and attention to detail.
5. Strong communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to present complex information clearly and effectively to various audiences.
6. Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively within a team environment.
7. Familiarity with banking regulations such as Dodd-Frank Act and CCAR guidelines is a plus.
8. CFA or similar professional designation is preferred.

This role offers an excellent opportunity for a motivated individual to grow their career in risk management and financial modeling within a dynamic organization. If you are passionate about risk analysis, have strong R programming skills, and enjoy working in a collaborative environment, we encourage you to apply.

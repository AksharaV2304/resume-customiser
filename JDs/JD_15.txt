 Job Title: Senior Financial Risk Analyst - IFRS9 Model Development

Location: [City, Country]

Company Overview:

Job Description:

The successful candidate will be responsible for the design, development, and implementation of IFRS9 models for [Company Name]. This role requires a deep understanding of financial risk management, accounting standards (specifically IFRS9), and advanced analytics using SAS.

Responsibilities:
1. Lead the development and implementation of IFRS9 models using SAS, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and company policies.
2. Collaborate with various stakeholders, including finance, risk management, and IT teams, to understand business needs and integrate model results into decision-making processes.
3. Perform data analysis, model validation, and stress testing to ensure the accuracy and reliability of models.
4. Develop and maintain documentation for all models, including technical specifications, assumptions, and limitations.
5. Stay abreast of industry trends, regulatory changes, and best practices in IFRS9 modeling and risk management.
6. Provide training and support to other team members on the use of SAS and IFRS9 models.
7. Participate in periodic model reviews and validation processes to ensure ongoing compliance and accuracy.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Economics, Mathematics, Statistics, or a related field. A professional qualification such as FRM, CFA, or ACT would be advantageous.
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in financial risk modeling, preferably within the retail sector.
3. Extensive knowledge of IFRS9 and its impact on financial institutions.
4. Advanced SAS programming skills with a proven track record of developing and implementing complex models.
5. Strong analytical skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
6. Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
7. Proficiency in SQL and experience with other data analysis tools is beneficial.

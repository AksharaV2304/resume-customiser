Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Wholesale Scorecards Model Validation Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in statistical analysis, data modeling, and risk management with a focus on the financial services sector.

Responsibilities:
1. Validate wholesale scorecard models using SAS programming language, ensuring the accuracy and reliability of the models.
2. Develop and implement model validation strategies, procedures, and standards for wholesale scorecards in compliance with regulatory requirements.
3. Collaborate with various teams (Risk Management, Compliance, IT) to ensure effective integration of scorecard models into business operations.
4. Conduct regular reviews and updates of existing scorecard models to reflect changes in data, market conditions, and regulatory landscape.
5. Prepare and present model validation reports to senior management, highlighting key findings, recommendations, and risks associated with the wholesale scorecards.
6. Participate in model development projects, providing guidance on best practices for model design, validation, and implementation.
7. Stay abreast of industry trends, regulatory changes, and emerging technologies related to risk modeling and scoring.
8. Contribute to the continuous improvement of our risk management framework by proposing and implementing enhancements to scorecard models, processes, and systems.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Finance, or a related field. Advanced degrees are preferred.
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in model validation, preferably within the financial services industry.
3. Expertise in SAS programming language and proficiency in other relevant statistical software packages (R, Python, etc.).
4. Strong understanding of credit risk modeling, scoring methodologies, and regulatory requirements.
5. Excellent analytical skills, with the ability to interpret complex data and draw meaningful insights.
6. Strong communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical audiences.
7. Proactive, results-oriented approach with a high level of attention to detail.
8. Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively in a team environment.
9. Knowledge of wholesale banking and products is a plus.

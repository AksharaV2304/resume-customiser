 Job Description: Retail Scorecard Model Auditor (R Specialization)

Position Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Retail Scorecard Model Auditor with proficiency in R programming to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for evaluating, validating, and improving the performance of our retail scorecard models.

Responsibilities:
1. Conduct thorough audits of existing retail scorecard models using R programming language.
2. Identify potential issues, inconsistencies, or biases within the models and propose solutions to improve their accuracy and effectiveness.
3. Develop new models as needed to address specific business challenges and opportunities in the retail industry.
4. Collaborate with data scientists, analysts, and other stakeholders to ensure model outcomes align with business objectives.
5. Document findings, recommendations, and implementation steps in a clear and concise manner for effective communication within the organization.
6. Stay up-to-date with industry trends, best practices, and advancements in retail analytics and R programming.
7. Contribute to the continuous improvement of our modeling processes and tools.

Qualifications:
1. Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Econometrics, Computer Science, or a related field.
2. Proven experience in retail data analysis and model auditing using R.
3. Strong understanding of statistical modeling techniques, machine learning algorithms, and predictive analytics.
4. Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work independently as well as collaboratively in a team environment.
5. Exceptional communication and presentation skills.
6. Proficiency in SQL for data extraction and manipulation is a plus.
7. Familiarity with other retail analytics tools (e.g., SAS, Python) is a bonus but not required.

By joining our team, you will have the opportunity to make a significant impact on our business and gain valuable experience in a growing and innovative company. We offer competitive compensation, a supportive work environment, and opportunities for professional development. If you are passionate about retail analytics and excited to leverage your skills using R programming, we encourage you to apply!

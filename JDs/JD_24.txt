Position Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Retail IFRS9 Model Audit Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in financial modeling, a deep understanding of IFRS9 regulations, and proficiency in SQL. This role is crucial for ensuring the accuracy and compliance of our retail financial models.

Responsibilities:
1. Conduct thorough audits of our retail IFRS9 models to ensure they comply with regulatory requirements.
2. Collaborate with the modeling team to identify, analyze, and resolve any discrepancies or issues found during audits.
3. Develop and implement best practices for model validation and ongoing monitoring.
4. Stay updated on industry trends, IFRS9 updates, and regulatory changes that may impact our models.
5. Utilize SQL to extract, manipulate, and analyze data from various sources for audit purposes.
6. Document findings, recommendations, and actions taken to ensure continuous improvement of the model auditing process.
7. Collaborate with other departments to ensure a unified approach to financial reporting and risk management.
8. Provide training and support to team members on IFRS9 regulations and model auditing best practices.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or a related field. A Master's degree or professional certification (CPA, FRM, etc.) is preferred.
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in financial modeling, preferably within the retail sector and with a focus on IFRS9.
3. Proficiency in SQL and strong analytical skills.
4. Knowledge of IFRS9 regulations and experience with model validation processes.
5. Strong problem-solving abilities and attention to detail.
6. Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
7. Ability to work independently and manage multiple tasks simultaneously.
8. Familiarity with risk management principles is a plus.

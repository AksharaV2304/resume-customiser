 Job Description: Retail Scorecard Model Validation Specialist

Company Overview:
[Company Name] is a dynamic and innovative retail organization seeking to expand its analytical team with a dedicated professional. Our company leverages the power of data to drive decision-making, improve customer experiences, and optimize business outcomes.

Position Overview:
As a Retail Scorecard Model Validation Specialist, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy, reliability, and regulatory compliance of our retail models using SAS. You will work closely with various departments to validate and enhance scorecards that are integral to our business operations.

Key Responsibilities:
1. Validate and maintain retail scorecard models using SAS programming language
2. Conduct thorough model testing, documentation, and reporting to ensure compliance with industry standards and regulatory requirements
3. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business needs and translate them into effective data analysis and modeling solutions
4. Identify areas for improvement and recommend enhancements to scorecard models to optimize their performance
5. Ensure adherence to best practices in model development, validation, and maintenance
6. Provide technical support and guidance on retail analytics initiatives as needed
7. Stay updated with industry trends, regulatory updates, and advancements in modeling techniques
8. Collaborate with management to implement process improvements and drive continuous improvement within the analytical function

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Economics, or a related field
2. Minimum of 3 years of experience in model validation, preferably in retail or a similar industry
3. Proficiency in SAS programming and statistical analysis techniques
4. Strong understanding of various modeling methodologies (e.g., logistic regression, decision trees, neural networks)
5. Knowledge of financial services regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, and GLBA is preferred
6. Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work effectively in a team environment
7. Strong communication skills with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders
8. Self-motivated and able to work independently as well as collaboratively
9. Results-driven with a commitment to delivering high-quality work and meeting project deadlines

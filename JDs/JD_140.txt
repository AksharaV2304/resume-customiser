 Job Title: Wholesale AI/ML Model Validation Specialist

Location: [Company Location]

Job Type: Full-Time

About the Role:

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Wholesale AI/ML Model Validation Specialist to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in data analysis, machine learning, and SQL, with a focus on ensuring the accuracy, reliability, and compliance of our AI/ML models in a wholesale context.

Responsibilities:

1. Validate AI/ML models used in our wholesale operations to ensure they meet business requirements, industry standards, and regulatory expectations.
2. Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and business stakeholders to understand model development processes and identify potential issues that may affect model performance or compliance.
3. Design, implement, and maintain SQL-based validation frameworks for AI/ML models, ensuring scalability, efficiency, and reusability.
4. Conduct thorough analysis of model outputs, identifying patterns, biases, and potential errors, and proposing corrective actions when necessary.
5. Develop and enforce best practices for model validation to ensure the quality and integrity of our AI/ML solutions.
6. Stay up-to-date with industry trends, regulatory changes, and emerging technologies in AI/ML validation, and incorporate these insights into our validation strategies.
7. Collaborate with other teams to integrate model validation processes into our overall data management and operational workflows.
8. Provide training and support to internal stakeholders on model validation best practices and techniques.
9. Participate in projects aimed at improving the accuracy, efficiency, and scalability of our AI/ML models.
10. Contribute to the continuous improvement of our AI/ML model validation processes and tools.

Qualifications:

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, or a related field. Advanced degrees are preferred.
2. Proven experience in AI/ML model validation, preferably in a wholesale context.
3. Strong proficiency in SQL and familiarity with other data manipulation languages.
4. Experience working with machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn.
5. Excellent analytical skills, with the ability to identify patterns, anomalies, and trends in complex datasets.
6. Strong problem-solving abilities and a data-driven approach to decision making.
7. Excellent communication skills, with the ability to explain complex concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences.
8. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
9. Familiarity with regulatory requirements related to AI/ML model validation is a plus.
10. Strong commitment to data integrity, quality, and security.

 Job Title: Senior AI/ML Model Auditor

Location: [Your Location]

Company Overview:
[Your Company Name] is a leading innovator in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning, committed to delivering cutting-edge solutions for businesses worldwide. We are currently seeking a Senior AI/ML Model Auditor with a strong focus on SAS to join our dynamic team.

Job Description:

As a Senior AI/ML Model Auditor, you will be responsible for ensuring the integrity and effectiveness of our AI/ML models by conducting comprehensive audits. You will work closely with various departments to understand their AI/ML needs, evaluate model performance, identify potential issues, and recommend improvements.

Responsibilities:

1. Conduct thorough audits of AI/ML models across multiple domains using SAS as the primary tool.
2. Analyze model performance metrics, data quality, and validation techniques to ensure compliance with industry standards.
3. Identify potential risks, biases, or errors in the models and develop corrective actions.
4. Collaborate with data scientists, AI/ML engineers, and business stakeholders to understand model requirements and provide guidance on best practices.
5. Document audit findings, recommendations, and implementation status for each project.
6. Stay abreast of industry trends, emerging technologies, and regulatory changes related to AI/ML model auditing.
7. Contribute to the development and improvement of our AI/ML model auditing framework and processes.

Qualifications:

1. Master's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, or a related field. A PhD is preferred.
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in AI/ML model auditing with a strong focus on SAS.
3. Proficient in using SAS for data analysis, model validation, and reporting.
4. Knowledge of machine learning algorithms, deep learning techniques, and statistical methods.
5. Strong understanding of AI/ML model lifecycle, from development to deployment to retirement.
6. Excellent communication skills with the ability to explain complex concepts in a simple and understandable manner.
7. Proactive, detail-oriented, and able to work independently as well as collaboratively.
8. Familiarity with regulatory requirements related to AI/ML model auditing (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) is a plus.

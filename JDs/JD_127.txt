 Job Title: Wholesale CCAR Model Validation Specialist

Location: [City, State]

Company Overview:
[Company Name] is a leading financial institution seeking a highly skilled and experienced Wholesale CCAR Model Validation Specialist to join our team. Key Responsibilities:
1. Validate and verify wholesale CCAR model outputs, assumptions, and methodologies to ensure accuracy, consistency, and compliance with regulatory requirements.
2. Collaborate closely with various business lines, risk management, and control functions to understand the underlying drivers of the models and identify potential risks or issues.
3. Utilize SAS programming language for data manipulation, model development, and validation tasks.
4. Document all validation procedures, findings, and recommendations in a clear and concise manner.
5. Participate in CCAR stress testing activities, including scenario analysis, backtesting, and sensitivity analysis.
6. Stay up-to-date with regulatory changes impacting CCAR model validation and ensure compliance within the wholesale business.
7. Collaborate with other model validation teams to share best practices and promote a strong risk management culture across the organization.
8. Support the development of internal model documentation and help in the preparation for regulatory examinations.
9. Contribute to the continuous improvement of our CCAR model validation processes, tools, and methodologies.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's degree in Finance, Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, or a related field; advanced degree preferred.
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in model validation or risk management within the financial services industry.
3. Proficiency in SAS programming language is essential.
4. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to understand complex models and data structures.
5. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to work effectively with various stakeholders.
6. Detail-oriented with strong organizational and project management skills.
7. Knowledge of CCAR regulations and stress testing methodologies is advantageous.
8. CFA or FRM certification is a plus.

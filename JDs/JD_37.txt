Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Retail Scorecard Model Developer with expertise in R programming to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing, developing, implementing, and maintaining retail scorecards that help drive business decisions, measure performance, and identify opportunities for improvement.

Responsibilities:
1. Develop, implement, and maintain retail scorecard models using R programming language.
2. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business needs and translate them into data-driven insights.
3. Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and relationships relevant to the retail industry.
4. Create user-friendly dashboards and visualizations for easy interpretation of the scorecard results.
5. Continuously improve existing models by incorporating new data sources and refining algorithms.
6. Provide recommendations on areas for improvement based on analysis findings.
7. Collaborate with IT teams to ensure models are integrated into our overall data infrastructure.
8. Stay up-to-date with industry trends, best practices, and advancements in retail analytics.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, Business Analytics, or a related field.
2. Proven experience (minimum 3 years) in developing and maintaining scorecard models, preferably within the retail industry.
3. Expertise in R programming language is mandatory. Proficiency in other analytics tools such as Python, SQL, SAS, and Tableau is a plus.
4. Strong analytical skills, with the ability to interpret complex data and draw meaningful conclusions.
5. Excellent communication skills, with the ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
6. Self-motivated and results-driven individual who thrives in fast-paced environments.
7. Passion for staying current with industry trends and advancements in data analytics.

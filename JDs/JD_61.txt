2. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business problems and define opportunities for model development and optimization.
3. Work closely with data engineers to ensure the availability and quality of data required for model building and testing.
4. Implement best practices in model development, including feature engineering, cross-validation, hyperparameter tuning, and model evaluation.
5. Document model architectures, assumptions, and performance metrics for knowledge sharing and transparency.
6. Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and business stakeholders to integrate models into production environments, ensuring scalability and reliability.
7. Continuously monitor and improve the performance of existing models, as well as develop new models to address evolving business needs.
8. Stay updated on industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in AI/ML model development and share insights with the team.
9. Participate in knowledge sharing sessions, workshops, and conferences to strengthen your expertise and contribute to the growth of the organization.
10. Mentor junior data scientists and support their professional development.

Qualifications:
1. Master's degree or higher in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, or a related field.
2. Proven experience (3+ years) in AI/ML model development, with a strong focus on R programming language.
3. Demonstrated proficiency in machine learning algorithms, deep learning architectures, and statistical methods.
4. Experience working on retail-related projects is highly desirable.
5. Strong problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work independently and collaboratively.
6. Excellent communication skills, with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
7. Passion for continuous learning and staying abreast of emerging technologies in AI/ML.
8. Strong team player who fosters a positive, collaborative environment.

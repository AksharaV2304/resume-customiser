 Job Description: Retail Credit Card Account Review (CCAR) Model Validation Specialist

Position Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Retail CCAR Model Validation Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in risk management, data analysis, and regulatory compliance with expertise in R programming. This role is essential for ensuring the accuracy, reliability, and compliance of our retail credit card models.

Responsibilities:
1. Validate and ensure the integrity of retail credit card models, adhering to CCAR guidelines and regulatory requirements.
2. Conduct thorough model risk assessments to identify potential issues and develop mitigation strategies.
3. Utilize R programming language to perform data analysis, statistical modeling, and simulations.
4. Collaborate with various stakeholders across the organization to understand business processes, data sources, and model assumptions.
5. Document validation results, findings, and recommendations in a clear and concise manner for senior management and regulatory bodies.
6. Stay updated on industry trends, best practices, and regulatory changes related to CCAR model validation.
7. Participate in ongoing monitoring and testing of models to ensure continued accuracy and compliance.
8. Contribute to the development and improvement of our CCAR model validation framework.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Mathematics, Economics, Statistics, or a related field.
2. Proven experience in retail credit card model validation, preferably within a banking or financial services environment.
3. Expertise in R programming and proficiency in other relevant software such as SAS, Python, or SQL.
4. Strong understanding of CCAR guidelines, risk management principles, and regulatory compliance requirements.
5. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
6. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
7. Attention to detail and commitment to maintaining high-quality work.
8. CFA, FRM, or similar professional certifications are preferred but not required.

We offer a competitive salary, comprehensive benefits, and opportunities for professional growth. If you are passionate about risk management and have a strong background in data analysis using R, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity!

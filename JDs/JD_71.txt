 Job Description: Retail AI/ML Model Auditor

Company Overview:
[Your Company Name] is a leading organization in the retail industry, leveraging advanced technologies to drive innovation and growth. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer experiences through our cutting-edge solutions.

Job Title: Retail AI/ML Model Auditor

Location: [Location]

About the Role:
We are seeking an experienced Retail AI/ML Model Auditor to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in data analytics, machine learning, and statistical modeling, with a focus on ensuring the integrity, accuracy, and transparency of our AI/ML models. SAS expertise is essential for this role.

Responsibilities:
1. Conduct comprehensive audits of our retail AI/ML models to identify potential biases, errors, or inconsistencies.
2. Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and business stakeholders to understand the underlying algorithms, assumptions, and data sources used in model development.
3. Develop and implement audit methodologies that ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations.
4. Perform root cause analysis on identified issues and recommend corrective actions.
5. Document findings and present them effectively to key stakeholders, including management, data scientists, and business teams.
6. Stay updated on the latest developments in AI/ML, data analytics, and regulatory requirements to continuously improve the audit process.
7. Contribute to the development of best practices for model governance within the organization.
8. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate auditing processes into the model development lifecycle.
9. Maintain a high level of confidentiality and data security when handling sensitive information.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, or a related field. Advanced degrees are preferred.
2. Proven experience in AI/ML model auditing, preferably within the retail industry.
3. Expertise in SAS and other data analytics tools. Familiarity with Python or R is also beneficial.
4. Strong understanding of machine learning algorithms, statistical modeling, and data mining techniques.
5. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
6. Ability to work independently as well as collaborate effectively within a team.
7. Commitment to maintaining the highest standards of integrity, confidentiality, and professionalism.
8. Knowledge of data privacy regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, and PIPEDA is a plus.

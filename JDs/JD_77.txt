 Job Description: Wholesale IRB Model Validation Specialist

Position Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Wholesale IRB Model Validation Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in financial modeling, statistical analysis, and risk management with a focus on regulatory compliance. Proficiency in R programming is essential for this role.

Responsibilities:
1. Validate wholesale IRB (Internal Ratings-Based) models used for credit risk assessment to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.
2. Collaborate with the model development team to understand the structure and assumptions of the IRB models.
3. Perform statistical analysis using R to assess model performance, robustness, and adherence to regulatory standards.
4. Develop and maintain documentation for model validation processes and results.
5. Participate in internal and external audits related to model validation.
6. Stay current with regulatory updates and industry best practices regarding IRB models.
7. Collaborate with other risk management functions to ensure the integrity of the credit risk assessment process.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in a quantitative discipline (e.g., Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Finance, Engineering).
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in model validation, preferably within a banking or financial institution.
3. Proficiency in R programming and statistical analysis.
4. Strong understanding of credit risk assessment methodologies and regulatory requirements (e.g., Basel III).
5. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
6. Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
7. Knowledge of other programming languages (e.g., Python, SAS) is a plus but not required.

We offer a competitive salary, comprehensive benefits, and opportunities for professional growth in a dynamic and collaborative environment. If you are passionate about financial modeling and regulatory compliance, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.

 Job Title: Retail Scorecards Model Developer

Location: [Your Location]

Job Summary:
We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Retail Scorecards Model Developer to join our Data Science & Analytics team. This role requires strong analytical skills, a deep understanding of data mining techniques, and the ability to work independently as well as collaboratively within cross-functional teams.

1. Develop and maintain retail scorecards models using Python and other relevant tools for various business applications such as credit risk assessment, customer segmentation, and marketing campaign optimization.
2. Collaborate with stakeholders to understand business requirements and translate them into technical specifications for model development.
3. Conduct exploratory data analysis to identify patterns and trends, and use these insights to improve existing models or develop new ones.
4. Implement machine learning algorithms and statistical models to build accurate and scalable scorecard solutions.
5. Validate models using appropriate techniques such as cross-validation, bootstrap resampling, and A/B testing.
6. Collaborate with other teams to ensure the successful integration of scorecards into existing business systems.
7. Continuously monitor model performance and make adjustments as necessary to maintain accuracy and relevance.
8. Document models and provide training materials for end-users.
9. Stay up-to-date with industry trends, best practices, and advancements in data science and machine learning.

3. Strong understanding of machine learning algorithms and statistical modeling techniques.
4. Experience with data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model evaluation.
5. Knowledge of database systems and SQL.
6. Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
7. Strong communication skills with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
8. Ability to work collaboratively within cross-functional teams.
9. Proactive, results-oriented, and passionate about data science and analytics.

 Job Description: Retail AI/ML Model Auditor

Position Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Retail AI/ML Model Auditor to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, with proficiency in Python as a must. This role involves reviewing and evaluating the performance, accuracy, fairness, and reliability of AI/ML models used in our retail operations.

Responsibilities:
1. Conduct comprehensive audits of AI/ML models to ensure they meet quality standards and comply with regulations.
2. Analyze model performance metrics, identify areas for improvement, and propose solutions to optimize model efficiency.
3. Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and business stakeholders to understand the underlying algorithms, data sources, and business objectives.
4. Assess and mitigate potential bias in AI/ML models to ensure fairness and non-discrimination.
5. Develop and implement strategies for continuous model monitoring and validation.
6. Stay abreast of industry trends, best practices, and advancements in AI/ML to continuously improve our models and processes.
7. Document findings, recommendations, and improvements in a clear and concise manner for various stakeholders.
8. Contribute to the development of internal policies and guidelines for responsible AI/ML model deployment.

Qualifications:
1. Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, or related field. A Ph.D. is preferred.
2. Extensive experience with Python, including libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, and PyTorch.
3. Proven experience in AI/ML model auditing, validation, and optimization.
4. Knowledge of machine learning algorithms, deep learning, and reinforcement learning.
5. Strong understanding of statistical analysis, data visualization, and predictive modeling.
6. Experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) is a plus.
7. Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, to effectively collaborate with various stakeholders.
8. Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously.

By joining our team, you will have the opportunity to work on cutting-edge AI/ML projects, collaborate with talented professionals, and make a significant impact on our retail operations. We are an equal opportunity employer and encourage applications from individuals of all backgrounds and abilities.

If you're passionate about AI/ML and eager to make a difference in the retail industry, we would love to hear from you!

 Title: Wholesale Scorecards Model Audit Specialist

Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Wholesale Scorecards Model Audit Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in quantitative analysis, risk management, and statistical modeling, with proficiency in the R programming language.

Responsibilities:

1. Conduct thorough audits of wholesale scorecard models used within the organization to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and internal policies.
2. Analyze and interpret large datasets using advanced statistical techniques and machine learning algorithms, primarily in the R programming language.
3. Identify potential issues or risks associated with current models and develop recommendations for improvements or modifications.
4. Collaborate with various departments across the organization to understand business needs and tailor models accordingly.
5. Document findings, recommendations, and any changes made to models in a clear and concise manner.
6. Stay updated on industry trends, regulations, and best practices related to scorecard modeling and risk management.
7. Provide support and guidance to other team members on model development, validation, and implementation.
8. Contribute to the continuous improvement of the scoring models, ensuring they remain effective and efficient in their intended use.

Qualifications:

1. Master's degree or higher in Mathematics, Statistics, Econometrics, Finance, or a related field.
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in risk management, quantitative analysis, or modeling roles within the financial services industry.
3. Proficiency in R programming language and statistical modeling techniques.
4. Strong understanding of credit risk, wholesale lending, and regulatory requirements.
5. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
6. Ability to work independently and collaboratively as part of a team.
7. Detail-oriented with strong organizational skills and the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
8. Knowledge of other programming languages (such as Python, SAS) is a plus.

If you are passionate about risk management, quantitative analysis, and leveraging data to drive business decisions, we would love to hear from you. Apply today to join our dynamic team!

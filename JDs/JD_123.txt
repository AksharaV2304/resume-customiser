As a Senior Wholesale CCAR Model Developer, you will be responsible for developing, maintaining, and enhancing wholesale Credit Risk Analytic Review (CCAR) models using SAS. * Collaborate with stakeholders to understand and address business requirements for CCAR modeling
* Validate and ensure the integrity of data used in CCAR models
* Participate in stress testing exercises and scenario analyses
* Provide support in model documentation and model validation processes
* Contribute to the continuous improvement of our CCAR models and processes

Qualifications:

* Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Econometrics, Computer Science, Finance, or a related field
* Minimum of 5 years of experience in wholesale CCAR model development using SAS
* Strong knowledge of credit risk modeling, stress testing, and regulatory requirements (DFAST, Dodd-Frank Act)
* Familiarity with various credit risk measures such as Expected Credit Losses (ECL), Value-at-Risk (VaR), and Economic Capital (EC)
* Proven ability to work collaboratively in a cross-functional team environment
* Excellent problem-solving skills and strong analytical abilities
* Strong communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills
* Validated experience with CCAR model development is preferred

 Job Title: Senior Retail Stress Testing Model Developer (R Specialization)

Location: [City, Country]

Company Overview:
[Company Name], a leading retail organization, is seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Retail Stress Testing Model Developer with a strong proficiency in R programming. The successful candidate will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining advanced stress testing models to support strategic decision-making and risk management within our organization.

Responsibilities:
1. Develop, maintain, and enhance retail stress testing models using R programming language.
2. Collaborate with business stakeholders to understand their requirements and translate them into actionable model specifications.
3. Implement statistical modeling techniques to analyze and forecast financial risk exposure under various scenarios.
4. Validate the accuracy of models, ensuring they comply with regulatory standards and internal policies.
5. Continuously improve models by incorporating feedback from business users and staying updated on industry best practices.
6. Provide support in model interpretation, reporting, and presenting results to senior management.
7. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate stress testing models into existing risk management processes.
8. Maintain documentation of all models and ensure that they are well-organized, easily accessible, and up-to-date.
9. Stay current on industry trends, new methodologies, and emerging technologies related to retail stress testing models.
10. Contribute to the development of best practices for retail stress testing model development within the organization.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Mathematics, Statistics, Econometrics, Computer Science, or a related field. A PhD is preferred.
2. Proven experience (minimum 5 years) in developing and maintaining stress testing models within the retail sector, with a strong focus on R programming.
3. Expertise in statistical modeling, time series analysis, and financial risk management.
4. Strong understanding of regulatory requirements related to stress testing, such as Basel III.
5. Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work independently as well as in a team environment.
6. Exceptional communication and presentation skills, with an ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
7. Strong project management skills, including the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and meet deadlines.
8. Knowledge of other programming languages such as Python or SQL is a plus.
9. Experience working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment is highly desirable.
10. Authorization to work in [Country] is required.

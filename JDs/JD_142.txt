We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Wholesale AI/ML Model Auditor to join our team. 1. Audit the design, development, testing, and deployment of AI/ML models in a wholesale context to ensure they meet best practices and regulatory requirements.
2. Perform comprehensive model validation, including assessing model performance, bias, fairness, explainability, and robustness.
3. Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and business stakeholders to understand the technical and business aspects of AI/ML models.
4. Develop and implement strategies for improving the quality and ethical implications of AI/ML models.
5. Document findings, recommendations, and best practices in clear and concise reports for clients and internal teams.
6. Stay up-to-date with the latest developments in AI/ML model auditing and regulatory requirements.
7. Contribute to the development of our AI/ML model auditing processes and tools.

Requirements:
1. Master's degree or higher in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics, or a related field.
2. Minimum 5 years of experience in AI/ML model auditing, data analysis, or similar roles.
3. Expertise in Python programming and proficiency in other relevant programming languages (e.g., R, Java).
4. Strong understanding of machine learning algorithms, deep learning, and statistical modeling.
5. Knowledge of regulatory requirements related to AI/ML models, such as GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA.
6. Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
7. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
8. Experience working with wholesale clients is a plus.

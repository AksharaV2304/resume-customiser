 Job Description: Retail AI/ML Model Validation Specialist

Company Overview:
[Company Name] is a dynamic and innovative organization in the retail sector, dedicated to leveraging technology to enhance customer experience and drive business growth. We are currently seeking a skilled and enthusiastic Retail AI/ML Model Validation Specialist to join our Data Science team.

Job Responsibilities:
1. Collaborate with data scientists, business analysts, and other stakeholders to validate the performance and robustness of AI/ML models developed for retail applications.
2. Utilize R programming language for model validation, statistical analysis, and reporting. Familiarity with other languages like Python or SQL is a plus but not mandatory as we primarily focus on R.
3. Develop and implement validation strategies to ensure the accuracy and reliability of machine learning models in predicting retail trends, customer behavior, and inventory management.
4. Conduct thorough analysis of model performance metrics such as precision, recall, F1 score, AUC-ROC, etc., to identify potential issues and opportunities for improvement.
5. Collaborate with the data engineering team to ensure seamless integration of validated models into our production systems.
6. Document all validation processes, findings, and recommendations in a clear and concise manner for easy understanding by stakeholders.
7. Stay updated on industry trends, best practices, and advancements in AI/ML model validation techniques.
8. Contribute to the continuous improvement of our data science practice by providing insights and suggestions for optimizing model performance and efficiency.
9. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure that validated models are aligned with business objectives and meet regulatory requirements.

Qualifications:
1. Master's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field. A PhD is preferred but not mandatory.
2. Minimum of 3 years of experience in AI/ML model validation, preferably within the retail sector.
3. Proficient in R programming language and familiarity with other data science tools such as Python, SQL, and TensorFlow.
4. Strong understanding of machine learning concepts, statistical analysis techniques, and model evaluation metrics.
5. Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work independently as well as part of a team.
6. Strong communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to explain complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
7. Passion for data science and AI/ML technology, with a commitment to continuous learning and improvement.

We offer a competitive salary package, flexible work hours, and opportunities for professional growth. If you are an innovative thinker who is passionate about using AI/ML to drive retail success, we would love to hear from you!

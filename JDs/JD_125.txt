 Job Title: Senior CCAR Model Validation Specialist

Location: [City, State]

Company Overview:
[Company Name], a leading financial institution, is seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior CCAR Model Validation Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in risk management, regulatory compliance, and quantitative analysis, with proficiency in R programming as a must.

Job Description:

The Senior CCAR Model Validation Specialist will be responsible for ensuring the accuracy, completeness, and regulatory compliance of capital planning models within [Company Name]. The primary focus will be on validating the Comprehensive Capital Analysis and Review (CCAR) model.

Responsibilities:

1. Conduct thorough validation of the CCAR model, including stress testing scenarios, model inputs, assumptions, outputs, and documentation.
2. Collaborate with various departments to understand model usage and ensure alignment with regulatory requirements.
3. Use R programming to automate model validations, perform data analysis, and create reports for stakeholders.
4. Participate in model development projects, providing guidance on validation best practices and model risk management.
5. Develop and maintain relationships with regulators, ensuring compliance with the latest CCAR guidelines and expectations.
6. Document all validation findings, issues, and recommendations in a clear and concise manner for stakeholders.
7. Contribute to the continuous improvement of the model validation process, identifying areas for enhancement and implementing changes as needed.

Qualifications:

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Mathematics, Economics, Statistics, or a related quantitative field. Advanced degrees are preferred.
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in model validation, risk management, or regulatory compliance within the financial services industry.
3. Proficiency in R programming is a must; experience with other statistical software such as SAS, Python, or SQL is a plus.
4. Strong understanding of capital planning models, stress testing methodologies, and regulatory requirements related to CCAR.
5. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
6. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
7. Proactive approach to identifying and addressing risks, with a focus on maintaining high-quality work products.
8. Knowledge of banking regulations, including CCAR guidelines and Fed stress tests.
9. Strong attention to detail, organizational skills, and the ability to manage multiple priorities effectively.
10. Authorized to work in the United States.

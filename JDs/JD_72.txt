 Job Description: Retail AI/ML Model Auditor

Position Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Retail AI/ML Model Auditor to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in artificial intelligence, machine learning, data analytics, and a deep understanding of SQL.

Responsibilities:
1. Conduct comprehensive audits of our retail AI/ML models to ensure they are functioning correctly, efficiently, and ethically.
2. Identify potential issues, biases, or discrepancies in the models and propose solutions to mitigate them.
3. Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and other stakeholders to understand the model's architecture, inputs, outputs, and business objectives.
4. Utilize SQL for data extraction, manipulation, and analysis to gain insights into the performance of AI/ML models.
5. Develop and implement best practices for model governance, transparency, and compliance with relevant regulations.
6. Contribute to the continuous improvement of our AI/ML models by providing recommendations based on audit findings and industry trends.
7. Collaborate with various teams to understand business requirements and translate them into technical specifications for model development and auditing.
8. Stay updated with the latest advancements in AI, ML, and data analytics, and apply this knowledge to improve our models and auditing processes.
9. Document audit findings and present them effectively to stakeholders, including senior management and technical teams.
10. Foster a culture of data-driven decision making by promoting the understanding and utilization of AI/ML models within the organization.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, or a related field.
2. Proven experience in auditing AI/ML models in a retail or similar industry.
3. Strong proficiency in SQL for data extraction, manipulation, and analysis.
4. Familiarity with AI/ML frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, etc.
5. Knowledge of statistical analysis and machine learning algorithms.
6. Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work independently and in a team environment.
7. Strong communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to explain complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
8. Self-motivated, detail-oriented, and able to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
9. Passionate about staying updated with AI/ML trends and best practices.

We offer a competitive salary, comprehensive benefits, opportunities for professional development, and a dynamic work environment. If you are passionate about AI/ML and want to make a significant impact on our retail operations, we encourage you to apply!

 Job Title: Senior Financial Risk Model Validator - IFRS9 Compliance (SAS Specialist)

Location: [City, State]

Company Overview:
[Company Name], a leading financial institution, is seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Financial Risk Model Validator to join our team. The ideal candidate will have strong technical expertise in IFRS9 regulatory compliance, specifically with SAS programming skills.

Job Description:

As a Senior Financial Risk Model Validator, you will play a critical role in ensuring the accuracy, robustness, and compliance of our wholesale IFRS9 model. Your primary responsibilities will include:

1. Conducting independent validation of the institution's IFRS9 models to ensure they are accurately capturing credit risk and conforming to regulatory requirements.
2. Collaborating with various business units, risk management, and regulatory departments to understand modeling methodologies, assumptions, and data inputs.
3. Developing, implementing, and maintaining SAS-based validation procedures for IFRS9 models, including stress testing and backtesting.
4. Identifying, documenting, and escalating any issues or risks related to model validity, compliance, or performance.
5. Participating in the development of new and enhanced models, providing expertise on IFRS9 regulations and best practices.
6. Staying up-to-date with evolving financial regulatory requirements and industry trends, particularly related to IFRS9.
7. Collaborating with internal and external auditors during model validation reviews.
8. Providing training and support to other team members on IFRS9 modeling and validation principles.

Qualifications:

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Mathematics, Economics, Statistics, or a related field. Advanced degrees are preferred.
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in financial risk management, with a focus on model validation, ideally within the banking sector.
3. Extensive knowledge of IFRS9 regulations and credit risk modeling principles.
4. Strong SAS programming skills and experience using SAS for model development, validation, and reporting.
5. Proven ability to communicate complex technical information effectively to various stakeholders.
6. Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
7. Self-motivated, adaptable, and able to work in a fast-paced environment.
8. CFA, FRM, or similar professional designation is preferred.

Job Description:

We are seeking a skilled and motivated Retail Stress Testing Model Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in financial modeling, data analysis, and Python programming. Responsibilities:

1. Design, develop, and implement retail stress testing models using Python and other relevant tools.
2. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather and validate data for model development.
3. Analyze the results of stress tests to identify potential risks and opportunities for our business.
4. Document models and results, providing clear explanations and interpretations.
5. Continuously improve models based on feedback and changing market conditions.
6. Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in retail risk management and financial modeling.
7. Provide support for ad-hoc analyses as needed.

Qualifications:

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Economics, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or a related field.
2. Proven experience in retail stress testing model development using Python.
3. Strong understanding of financial markets, risk management, and statistical analysis.
4. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
5. Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
6. Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, particularly Excel.
7. Knowledge of other programming languages (e.g., SQL, R) is a plus.
8. Experience with financial databases and APIs is preferred.
9. CFA or FRM certification is a plus but not required.

2. Develop and implement validation plans for new and existing models, including identification of key risks and validation methods.
3. Utilize Python to build scripts and tools for model testing, analysis, and documentation.
4. Collaborate with various stakeholders across the organization to understand business processes and ensure model outputs align with expected results.
5. Document validation findings, recommendations, and actions taken to address identified issues.
6. Stay updated on industry developments, regulatory changes, and best practices in model validation.
7. Contribute to the continuous improvement of the model validation process, promoting efficiency and effectiveness.

2. Proven experience in model validation, preferably within a banking or financial services environment. Experience with Wholesale IRB models is highly desirable.
3. Proficiency in Python and other programming languages for data manipulation, analysis, and modeling.
4. Strong understanding of risk management principles, banking regulations, and industry best practices.
5. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
6. Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team environment.
7. Attention to detail, with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
8. Regulatory certification (e.g., PRMIA, FRM) is a plus.

 Job Description: Retail IRB Model Auditor

Company Overview:
[Company Name] is a leading organization in the retail sector, committed to innovation and excellence. We are currently seeking a skilled and experienced Retail IRB Model Auditor to join our team.

Job Responsibilities:
1. Conduct comprehensive audits of our Retail IRB (Institutional Review Board) models, ensuring compliance with industry standards, regulatory requirements, and company policies.
2. Utilize SAS (Statistical Analysis Software) for data analysis, modeling, and reporting during the audit process. Familiarity with other statistical software is a plus but not mandatory as long as you are proficient in SAS.
3. Identify areas of improvement and make recommendations for enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of our IRB models.
4. Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including data scientists, statisticians, and compliance officers, to ensure seamless integration of audit findings and recommendations.
5. Document audit findings, create reports, and present results to senior management.
6. Stay updated on industry trends, regulations, and best practices related to IRB models in retail.
7. Participate in ongoing training and development programs to maintain a high level of expertise in SAS and IRB auditing.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, or a related field. Advanced degrees are preferred.
2. Proven experience in conducting IRB model audits, preferably within the retail sector.
3. Expertise in SAS for data analysis and modeling.
4. Strong understanding of statistical methods, data mining, and machine learning techniques.
5. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
6. Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
7. Familiarity with retail industry regulations, particularly those related to IRB models, is a plus.

[Company Name] offers a competitive salary and benefits package, along with opportunities for growth and professional development. If you are passionate about data analysis, have a strong background in SAS, and are eager to make an impact in the retail sector, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.

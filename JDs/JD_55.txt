 Job Description: Retail Credit Risk Model Validation Specialist

Company Overview:
[Company Name] is a leading retail organization seeking a highly skilled and experienced Retail Credit Risk Model Validation Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in credit risk, statistical modeling, and regulatory compliance, with proficiency in SAS being a must.

Job Responsibilities:

1. Validate the retail Credit Conditions Assessment and Review (CCAR) models to ensure they are accurate, complete, and comply with regulatory standards.

2. Collaborate with various stakeholders, including model developers, risk managers, and regulators, to understand the models and identify any potential issues or risks.

3. Develop and implement testing strategies for the CCAR models, utilizing SAS for data analysis and reporting.

4. Evaluate model assumptions, inputs, outputs, and methodologies to ensure they are reasonable, consistent, and aligned with regulatory guidelines.

5. Document findings, recommendations, and any necessary changes or improvements to the models and processes.

6. Stay updated on industry trends, regulatory requirements, and best practices in credit risk modeling and validation.

7. Contribute to the continuous improvement of the model validation process, ensuring it remains effective, efficient, and robust.

8. Ensure compliance with relevant regulations, such as the Federal Reserve's Supervision and Regulation (SR) 23-7, "Model Risk Management Guidelines."

9. Provide support during regulatory examinations and audits related to CCAR model validation.

Qualifications:

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Finance, or a related quantitative field.

2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in credit risk modeling, model validation, or a similar role within the financial services industry.

3. Proficiency in SAS for data analysis and reporting is essential. Knowledge of other programming languages (e.g., R, Python) is a plus.

4. Strong understanding of credit risk management principles and regulatory requirements, particularly those related to CCAR.

5. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.

6. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.

7. Detail-oriented with strong organizational skills and the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.

8. Strong understanding of statistical techniques and their application in credit risk modeling.

[Company Name] offers competitive compensation, comprehensive benefits, and opportunities for professional growth and development. If you are a motivated individual with a passion for credit risk and model validation, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.

 Job Title: Senior Wholesale IRB Model Validation Specialist

Location: [Your City/State]

Company Overview:
[Your Company Name] is a leading financial institution with a strong commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation. We are currently seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Wholesale IRB Model Validation Specialist to join our Risk Management team.

Job Description:

The Senior Wholesale IRB Model Validation Specialist will play a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy and reliability of our Internal Ratings-Based (IRB) models, specifically for wholesale lending products. The successful candidate will have a strong understanding of risk management principles, a deep knowledge of SQL, and exceptional analytical skills.

Responsibilities:

1. Conduct independent model validations of the IRB models used in our wholesale lending business, ensuring they comply with regulatory requirements and best practices.
2. Utilize advanced statistical techniques and SQL for data analysis, model development, and validation.
3. Collaborate with various stakeholders across the organization to understand business processes, data quality, and model assumptions.
4. Prepare comprehensive validation reports detailing model performance, risks, and recommendations for improvements.
5. Stay abreast of regulatory changes impacting IRB models and ensure our models are aligned with these changes.
6. Participate in model change approvals and provide expert advice on model risk management.
7. Contribute to the development and enhancement of model validation processes and methodologies.

Requirements:

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Finance, or a related field. Advanced degrees are preferred.
2. Minimum of 5 years of experience in model validation, preferably within a banking or financial services environment.
3. Extensive knowledge of IRB models and wholesale lending products.
4. Proficiency in SQL and strong analytical skills.
5. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
6. Strong problem-solving abilities and attention to detail.
7. Knowledge of banking regulations, particularly those related to model risk management.
8. Certified in Financial Risk Manager (FRM) or similar qualifications is a plus.

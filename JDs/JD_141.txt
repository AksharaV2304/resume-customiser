 Job Title: Wholesale AI/ML Model Auditor (R Specialization)

Company Overview:
[Company Name] is a leading innovator in the tech industry, specializing in advanced artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) solutions. We are currently seeking an experienced and highly skilled Wholesale AI/ML Model Auditor with expertise in R programming to join our dynamic team.

Job Description:

As a Wholesale AI/ML Model Auditor, you will be responsible for reviewing, assessing, and validating the performance, accuracy, and ethical implications of AI/ML models developed by our wholesale partners. Your primary focus will be on ensuring that the AI/ML models meet industry standards, comply with regulations, and align with our company's values.

Key Responsibilities:
1. Perform thorough audits of AI/ML models developed by our wholesale partners using R programming language.
2. Analyze model performance metrics, including accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 score.
3. Evaluate the robustness, fairness, and explainability of the AI/ML models.
4. Identify any potential issues or risks associated with the models and suggest improvements or remediation strategies.
5. Collaborate with our wholesale partners to address identified issues and ensure the continuous improvement of their AI/ML models.
6. Stay updated on the latest developments in AI/ML, data science, and related regulatory requirements.
7. Document your findings and communicate them effectively to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, or a related field. Advanced degrees are preferred.
2. Proven experience as an AI/ML Model Auditor or similar role, preferably within the wholesale sector.
3. Expertise in R programming and strong understanding of machine learning algorithms, data mining techniques, and statistical analysis.
4. Familiarity with AI/ML model validation methodologies and best practices.
5. Experience working with regulatory compliance requirements related to AI/ML models.
6. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
7. Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively in a team environment.
8. Strong attention to detail, ethical awareness, and a commitment to maintaining high standards of quality.

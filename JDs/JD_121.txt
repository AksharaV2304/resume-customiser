Location: [Your Location]

Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Wholesale CCAR Model Development Specialist to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for developing and maintaining high-quality risk models that support capital requirements under the Capital Adequacy Risk (CCAR) framework, focusing on wholesale business lines.

Key Responsibilities:

1. Develop, maintain, and enhance wholesale CCAR models using R programming language, ensuring accuracy, robustness, and compliance with regulatory guidelines.
2. Collaborate with business stakeholders to understand their risk profiles and incorporate relevant data into the model.
3. Perform regular model validation, stress testing, and backtesting to ensure model integrity and reliability.
4. Participate in CCAR submission processes, including document preparation, model validation, and management of model change requests.
5. Contribute to the development of best practices for model design, documentation, and governance.
6. Stay current with regulatory developments and industry trends related to risk modeling and capital requirements.
7. Provide support and guidance to junior team members in model development and maintenance activities.

Qualifications:

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Mathematics, Economics, Statistics, or a related field. Advanced degrees (MSc, PhD) are preferred.
2. Proven experience in wholesale risk modeling, with a strong focus on CCAR models using R programming language.
3. Strong understanding of risk management principles and regulatory requirements for capital adequacy.
4. Excellent analytical skills, with the ability to interpret complex data and identify key drivers of risk.
5. Exceptional problem-solving abilities and attention to detail.
6. Strong communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical audiences.
7. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
8. Knowledge of the banking industry, preferably within a wholesale or capital markets context.
9. Familiarity with CCAR submission processes and regulatory reporting requirements is a plus.
10. Certified Financial Risk Manager (FRM) or Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) designation is preferred but not required.

This is an exciting opportunity for a motivated and experienced risk modeler to join a dynamic team focused on driving the success of our wholesale business. If you are passionate about risk management, have strong analytical skills, and enjoy working in a challenging and innovative environment, we would love to hear from you.

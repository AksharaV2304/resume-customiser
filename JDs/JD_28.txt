Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Retail Stress Testing Model Developer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in financial modeling, data analysis, and SQL programming.

Responsibilities:

1. Develop and implement stress testing models for retail businesses, including scenario analysis, sensitivity analysis, and Monte Carlo simulations.

2. Collaborate with business stakeholders to understand and quantify risks associated with various retail scenarios.

3. Design, code, and maintain SQL databases to store financial data, model outputs, and parameters.

4. Analyze and interpret large datasets using SQL queries and statistical methods to derive insights and inform the development of stress testing models.

5. Validate and document stress testing models, ensuring they are robust, accurate, and align with company policies and industry standards.

6. Collaborate with other teams to integrate stress testing models into the overall risk management framework.

7. Stay abreast of developments in retail finance and stress testing methodologies, and incorporate best practices into model development.

Requirements:

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Economics, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or a related field.

2. Proven experience in developing financial models, preferably in the retail sector.

3. Strong proficiency in SQL and programming languages such as Python or R.

4. Knowledge of stress testing methodologies, risk management principles, and financial regulations (e.g., Basel III).

5. Excellent analytical skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work independently and collaboratively.

6. Strong communication skills, with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.

7. A passion for continuous learning and staying current with industry trends and developments.

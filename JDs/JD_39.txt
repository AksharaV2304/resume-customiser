Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Retail Scorecard Model Developer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in data analysis, statistical modeling, and retail industry experience. SAS programming skills are essential for this role.

Responsibilities:

1. Design, develop, and implement retail scorecard models using SAS programming language.
2. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business needs and translate them into analytical requirements.
3. Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and insights that drive strategic decision making.
4. Develop and maintain scorecards, dashboards, and reports to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs).
5. Implement best practices in data quality control, model validation, and model maintenance.
6. Continuously improve the accuracy and efficiency of existing models through testing, optimization, and automation.
7. Provide expert advice on model interpretations, results, and recommendations to stakeholders.
8. Collaborate with IT teams to ensure seamless integration of scorecard models into our data infrastructure.
9. Stay updated with the latest trends and developments in retail analytics and SAS programming.

Qualifications:

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Economics, Business Analytics, or a related field.
2. Proven experience in developing and implementing scorecard models, preferably within the retail industry.
3. Expertise in SAS programming language.
4. Strong understanding of statistical analysis techniques, machine learning algorithms, and predictive modeling.
5. Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work independently as well as collaboratively.
6. Exceptional communication skills with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
7. Strong attention to detail and a results-oriented mindset.
8. Familiarity with SQL or other database query languages is a plus.

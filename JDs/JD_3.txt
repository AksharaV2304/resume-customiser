Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Retail IRB (Individualized Risk-Based) Model Development Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in statistical modeling, risk assessment, and retail banking regulations. SAS proficiency is a must.

Responsibilities:

1. Develop, implement, and maintain the Retail IRB model for credit risk assessment, using advanced analytical techniques and SAS programming.
2. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather data, define business requirements, and validate model outcomes.
3. Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements, industry best practices, and internal policies.
4. Continuously improve the model by incorporating new data sources, refining algorithms, and implementing feedback loops for ongoing monitoring and validation.
5. Provide expert advice on model-related matters to stakeholders, including management, risk management, and operational teams.
6. Collaborate with IT to ensure model integration within the existing technology infrastructure.
7. Participate in stress testing, backtesting, and scenario analysis to validate model performance under various market conditions.
8. Contribute to thought leadership by publishing articles, presenting at conferences, and participating in industry forums.

Qualifications:

1. Master's degree or higher in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, Finance, or a related field.
2. Proven experience in retail banking, with a focus on credit risk modeling, preferably using SAS.
3. Strong understanding of regulatory requirements for credit risk modeling in retail banking (e.g., Basel III, Dodd-Frank Act).
4. Expertise in statistical and econometric modeling techniques, such as logistic regression, decision trees, neural networks, and survival analysis.
5. Excellent problem-solving skills, with the ability to analyze complex data sets and interpret results effectively.
6. Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
7. Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, manage multiple projects simultaneously, and meet deadlines.
8. Knowledge of agile development methodologies is a plus.

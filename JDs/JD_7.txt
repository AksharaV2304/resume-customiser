 Job Description: Retail IRB Model Validation Specialist

Company Overview:
[Company Name] is a leading retail organization with a strong commitment to innovation and excellence. We are seeking a talented and experienced Retail IRB Model Validation Specialist to join our team.

Job Role:
The Retail IRB Model Validation Specialist will be responsible for ensuring the accuracy, reliability, and compliance of our risk models within the retail banking sector. The ideal candidate should have a strong background in statistical analysis, risk management, and regulatory compliance.

Key Responsibilities:
1. Validate and ensure the integrity of IRB model outputs using appropriate validation methodologies and techniques.
2. Conduct sensitivity analyses to assess the robustness of models under various stress tests and scenarios.
3. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements, data needs, and regulatory expectations.
4. Ensure compliance with relevant regulatory guidelines, such as Basel III and CCAR, regarding model validation.
5. Utilize SAS for statistical analysis, modeling, and reporting purposes.
6. Document all validation activities in a clear, concise manner to ensure transparency and traceability.
7. Provide ongoing support for model maintenance, including identifying and implementing improvements to enhance model performance.
8. Stay abreast of industry developments and regulatory updates related to risk modeling and validation.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Finance, or a related field.
2. Proven experience in model validation, preferably within the retail banking sector.
3. Expertise in SAS for statistical analysis and modeling.
4. Strong understanding of risk management principles and regulatory requirements.
5. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
6. Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment and manage multiple tasks effectively.
7. Self-motivated with a strong attention to detail and the ability to work independently.

[Company Name] offers a competitive salary package, a supportive work environment, and opportunities for professional growth. If you are passionate about risk modeling and validation, and have a strong proficiency in SAS, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.

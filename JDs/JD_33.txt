 Job Description: Retail Stress Testing Model Auditor

Company Overview:
[Company Name] is a leading retail organization seeking an experienced and highly skilled Retail Stress Testing Model Auditor. The successful candidate will play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity, accuracy, and compliance of our risk management models.

Responsibilities:
1. Conduct thorough audits of retail stress testing models, identifying potential issues or areas for improvement.
2. Utilize R programming language extensively to analyze and manipulate data, develop models, and perform stress tests.
3. Collaborate with various departments to understand business requirements and ensure model outputs align with expectations.
4. Assess the robustness of models under different scenarios, including adverse market conditions.
5. Document findings and recommendations in a clear and concise manner, providing actionable insights to improve model performance.
6. Stay updated on industry best practices, regulatory guidelines, and emerging trends in retail risk management.
7. Participate in the development of internal policies and procedures related to stress testing models.

Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Finance, or a related field. Advanced degrees are preferred.
2. Proven experience in auditing or validating risk management models within the retail sector.
3. Expertise in R programming language is essential; knowledge of other languages such as Python and SAS is a plus.
4. Strong understanding of stress testing methodologies, risk management principles, and financial markets.
5. Excellent analytical skills, attention to detail, and problem-solving abilities.
6. Exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal.
7. Ability to work independently as well as collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
8. Experience in a regulated environment is preferred but not required.
9. Familiarity with regulatory guidelines such as Basel III, CCAR, and DFAST is a plus.
